Races

To begin bulding your character, choose their race. The races are divided by continent of origin, so be sure you know where the area of play is. Each race will contain the stats for that race, a list of subraces if applicable, and some information on the lore of the race with links to further reading.

Classes

The next step in creating your character is to choose a class. You can think of your class as a combination of your character's skill set and place in the world. Its an expression of how your character interacts with the world around them as an adventurer.

Each class has a series of sublcasses that change or add to the main class in different ways. You will choose one subclass at the appropriate level. Be sure to refer to the class table in each class main page to see what your character gains each level.
