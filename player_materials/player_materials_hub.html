Each race will contain the stats for that race, a list of subraces if applicable, and some information on the lore of the race with links to further reading.

Classes

The next step in creating your character is to choose a class. You can think of your class as a combination of your character's skill set and place in the world. Its an expression of how your character interacts with the world around them as an adventurer.

Each class has a series of sublcasses that change or add to the main class in different ways. You will choose one subclass at the appropriate level. Be sure to refer to the class table in each class main page to see what your character gains each level.

History

A character's history is made up of their background and backstory. Your character's background depicts the kind of life your character lived before they got to the point where the campaign begins. This life is not necessarily in the character's past and may still effect them throughout the campaign, or not.

Their backstory is essentially their lifestory, written by you. It may be long or short, limited only by your imagination, though I ask you keep a maximum of 5 pages in mind.

Feats

Feats are essentially special abilities That can enhance your character's combat ability, give you access to skills you wouldn't otherwise be able to get, or simply add additional flavor your character. You start with one at Level 1, and you can gain more whenever you would get an Ability Score Improvement. You take either the Ability Score Improvement or the Feat.

Equipment

The equipment your character carries can be bought here. Currency is organized in a 4-tier system: copper being the least valuable, silver converts to 10 copper pieces, gold converts to 10 silver pieces, and platinum converts to 10 gold pieces. You start with 70 gold pieces plus any additional money you gain from your background. You can spend this money on your starting equipment found below.

Spells

Magic is esoteric and rare in the world of Ouroboros. If you are playing as a class that uses magic, you may face exaltation or persecution depending on where you are or who you encounter. It is commonly said that magic flows from the soul through pathways in the blood and is shaped by the mind. There is generally an inheritable element to magic ability though this can be circumvented with dangerous augmentations or the aid of a supernatural being. Generally, magic is split into three categories: Arcane magic represent traditional casting and draws upon the power of the soul, Divine magic represents gifts from the gods to their most devout followers, and Eldritch magic comes from more ineffable and obscure sources.
