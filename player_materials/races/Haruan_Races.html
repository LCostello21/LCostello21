Haru is a culturally diverse continent with many different tribes forming before the Age of Horizons on the many islands of the continent. With the invention of boats to sail the seas and kites to soar amongst the clouds, through war, strife, piety, trade, and circumstance three great nations have emerged: The Eternal Dynasty on the Island of Zhen, the Ashen Shogunate on the island of Haishima, and the Free Cities on the island of Gyano. Each species is categorized under the Nation they originate from, though due to the expansion and accessabilitty of transportation your character need not have been born or have lived within that nation. In addition to choosing your species, you may also choose an origin.
