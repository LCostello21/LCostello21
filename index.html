Intro

Ouroboros is a high-fantasy world of mystery, wonder, the struggles of human nature, and cosmic horror. Nations will rise and fall in the shadows of bickering gods, and the advancement of science and magic will unveil new and disturbing questions on the nature of the universe.

"As I touched the silvery, glowing stone my mind began to expand beyond the confines of my body. I could feel the life pulse of other creatures around me, their hearts beating, their hunger, their dreams. My initial fascination shriveled to dread as the expansion of my conscience continued, and I began to become...aware...of a connection within the life around me, a being, or perhaps a pattern my straining mind could only begin to comprehend. I'm lucky I managed to pull away, else I fear I'd have seen too much and been lost." - notes from the journal of Bruklad Silverfingers, 302 ED

Making a Character

The Player Materials page has all you will need to make a character. However, knowing the lore of the part of the world you will be playing in will greatly aid both you the player and your DM when creating your backstory and integrating your character into the world. You don't have to know everything, just enough to understand your position in the world. The information in the Lore section is presented as "common knowledge," basically what a regular person might know on the topic. Advanced information is located in the DM Materials page and is to be read at your DM's discretion.
